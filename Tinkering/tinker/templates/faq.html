What are the timings for the lab? The Lab timings are from 11 am – 7 pm from Monday to Friday. Currently, the lab is operational for 5 days a week.

What procedure do I need to follow to access the Lab’s Facilities? Students’ have to first register on the Tinkering Lab portal, then they have to do a login for filing a request which requires the information of the project.

Can I use the lab for personal use? If your personal use is a personal project you are most welcome. Tinkering Lab doesn’t appreciate any kind of misuse of the Lab facilities and its facilities to cater personnel needs.

What are the facilities present at the Lab? The Tinkering Lab is equipped with numerous technologies including 3D printing, laser etching, carving and with number of workstations.

I don’t know how to use the machines so who will help me to use them? The lab officials and students’ body is meant to guide the students in their projects. They are always available for the help. Also, over a period of time, you will develop the skills of using these machines.

Who can all access the lab? The lab is present cater to the need of students and professors of IIT Roorkee. Any member of IIT Roorkee can access the lab for the projects.

Are there any prerequisites for working in the lab? No, there are no such prerequisites for working in the lab but we appreciate if you come in formals to keep yourself safe from any kind of mishappening in the lab.

Whom can I contact for further queries? Tinkering Lab is always present at your service. For further queries, feel free to drop an email at rethink.tinkering@gmail.com .
